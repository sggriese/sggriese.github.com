About Me

I'm a videographer and editor who thoroughly enjoys taking on new projects. I've worked as a Production Assistant on a US Bank Corporate video shoot, and I've independently produced promotional videos for Roaring Lion Energy Drink and FLY Fashion School at Florence University of the Arts. I've also independently produced music videos and paintball edits for clients around the Minneapolis/St. Paul metro area.

I've always taken an initiative to start things and get them done. While I was in high school I earned an Eagle Scout award and planned a three-week, 5,000 mile road trip for myself and eight friends. In my senior year I received an art scholarship to study film at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee.

After pursuing my degree for three years, I've decided to take an opportunity to move out to San Francisco and work on a website with a good friend of mine. I want to stay active with my passion for film while I'm here, and now I'm looking for work that gives me a challenge and brings out my creativity. If you'd like to work with me, let me know.

Sabotage at the Appel Farm

While working in New Jersey at the Appel Farm Art Camp, I lived in a bunk with five kids and taught them how to make music videos. We recreated Spike Jonze’s video for Sabotage by the Beastie Boys, except I didn’t let them use knives or kill people.
